Psychology test

A psychology test is being developed for students to assess their personality traits. One of the tasks involves the identification of the most common word used by the individual, as this can provide insight into their thinking process. Your task is to develop a function that receives a sentence and returns the most used word in the sentence.

Task

Develop a function that receives a sentence and returns the most used word in the sentence.

Input

A string representing a sentence

Output

A string representing the most used word in the sentence

Example

Input

"The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog"

Output

"the"
