FizzBuzz

FizzBuzz is a popular programming puzzle that is often used in programming interviews to test basic programming skills. The task is simple, yet can be challenging for some beginners. It involves printing out numbers from 1 to a given number, and replacing multiples of 3 with 'Fizz', multiples of 5 with 'Buzz', and multiples of both 3 and 5 with 'FizzBuzz'.

Task

Create a program that takes in a number and prints out a sequence of numbers from 1 to that number, replacing multiples of 3 with 'Fizz', multiples of 5 with 'Buzz', and multiples of both 3 and 5 with 'FizzBuzz'.

Input

The input should be a positive integer greater than 0.

Output

The output should be a sequence of numbers and/or strings, separated by spaces.

Example

Input

"15"

Output

"1 2 Fizz 4 Buzz Fizz 7 8 Fizz Buzz 11 Fizz 13 14 FizzBuzz"
